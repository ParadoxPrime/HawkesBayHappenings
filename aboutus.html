About Hawkes Bay Happenings


Welcome to Hawkes Bay Happenings — where a bunch of sleep-deprived students decided that sunshine, vineyards, and good coffee weren’t enough… so we added ghosts. We’re a creative team of local students with a passion for storytelling, folklore, and things that go bump in the night. What started as a class project quickly spiraled into something a little weirder — a digital campfire for all the eerie, funny, and downright bizarre tales lurking around Hawkes Bay, New Zealand.

So thanks for stopping by! Stick around, explore the Bay’s shadowy side, and remember — it’s all mostly fiction… probably.
